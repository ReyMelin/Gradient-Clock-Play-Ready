Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2026

Overview

Gradient Clock is a privacy-first clock application that displays the current time through colorful, animated rings in the app and as a home screen widget. Your privacy is our top priority.

No Data Collection

Gradient Clock does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The application:

  • Does not collect any user data whatsoever
  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not track your activity
  • Does not access your location
  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not make any network requests
  • Works completely offline

Local Storage (Device Only)

The app stores your display preferences locally on your device only to remember your settings between sessions:

  • Time format preference (24-hour or AM/PM)
  • Theme selection (metallic, winter, etc.)
  • Clock marker style (roman numerals, dots, etc.)

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party. You can clear these preferences at any time through your device's app settings.

Third-Party Services

Gradient Clock does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or tracking tools of any kind.

Time and Timezone

The application accesses your device's system time solely to display the clock. Your device's timezone setting is used automatically to show your local time. This information never leaves your device and is not stored or transmitted in any way.

Home Screen Widget

The app includes an optional home screen widget that displays the current time with a gradient background. The widget:

  • Updates periodically using Android's WorkManager (system service, not a third-party)
  • Uses your device's local time and timezone settings
  • Generates gradient colors based on the time of day
  • Does not collect, store, or transmit any data
  • Works completely offline

Permissions

The Android app requests minimal permissions:

  • Keep screen awake (WAKE_LOCK): Prevents your screen from dimming while viewing the clock in the app

No other permissions are requested or used. Location services are explicitly disabled. The widget does not require any special permissions.

Background Processing

If you add the widget to your home screen, the app uses Android's WorkManager to schedule periodic updates (approximately every 15 minutes) to keep the widget display current. This is a standard Android system service and does not collect or transmit any data.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
